# CILANTRO LIME SALSA

**Serves:** 10 | **Prep:** 5 MINS | **Cook:** N/A

## Macros

| Calories | Fat | Carbs | Net Carbs | Protein |
|----------|-----|-------|-----------|---------|
| 20 | 1 | 1 | undefined | 2 |

## Ingredients

- 224g fat-free Greek yogurt
- 30g light mayonnaise
- 4g garlic, minced
- Zest of 1 lime
- 16g lime juice
- 30g fresh cilantro, chopped
- 3g Valentina hot sauce
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

## Directions

1. Add all ingredients to a food processor and process for 30 seconds.
2. Scrape down sides with spatula and process for an additional 30 seconds.
3. Pour salsa into a sauce bottle and serve right away or refrigerate for a couple hours for peak flavor.

## Tips

Use within 1-2 weeks or before ingredients are set to expire.
