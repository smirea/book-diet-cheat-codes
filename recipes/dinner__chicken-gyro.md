# CHICKEN GYRO

**Serves:** 5 | **Prep:** 1.5 HRS | **Cook:** 30 MINS

## Macros

| Calories | Fat | Carbs | Net Carbs | Protein |
|----------|-----|-------|-----------|---------|
| 264 | 7 | 23 | 17 | 33 |

## Ingredients

- 4 boneless skinless chicken thighs

### CHICKEN MARINADE

- 3g fresh parsley, chopped
- 15g white vinegar
- 5g garlic, minced
- 30g lemon juice
- 60g fat-free Greek yogurt
- 6g extra virgin olive oil
- 4g smoked paprika
- 6g salt
- 3g MSG
- 3g black pepper

### CHICKEN GYRO BUILD

- 1 PROTEIN PITA
- 63g cooked chicken
- 60g TZATZIKI SAUCE
- 7g fat-free feta
- 2 slices tomato
- 6 slices onion
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Using a cutting board, trim excess fat off the chicken.
2. Add Chicken Marinade ingredients to a medium bowl, whisk until combined, add chicken, and make sure every piece is thoroughly coated.
3. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for one hour 
— up to 24 hours for maximum flavor.
4. When ready to eat, arrange chicken on a sheet pan lined with parchment paper and a wire rack and bake in a preheated 400°F oven for 20-30 minutes or until internal temperature of the chicken reaches 155°F.
5. Chop tomatoes and onion. Once chicken is finished, slice into bite sized pieces.
6. Microwave a pita for 10 seconds and layer it with chicken, tzatziki sauce, feta, tomato, onion, and parsley.
7. Repeat step 6 for any additional servings and it's time to eat.
